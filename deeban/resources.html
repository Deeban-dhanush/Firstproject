Department of BCA :

The Department of BCA was established in the year 2014 under the auspices of Faculty of Science and Humanities with an intake of 65 students. It makes every effort to develop world class self disciplined graduates in computer applications who will be emerging as Social/Computer Technocrat and holding the responsibility of enriching the Technical and Research status of our Nation. The Department of BCA has an excellent scholastic ambience and infrastructure for the successful conduct of the bachelor’s programme in computer applications. The key to this effort is the dexterity among the faculty, staff and the students. This success is attributed to the dedicated and responsible faculty members of our department who imparts quality education.


Department of BSc :

The Department of Computer Science was established in the year 2015 under the auspices of Faculty of Science and Humanities It strives hard to develop world class, self disciplined computer professionals who will be responsible for enriching the economical status of our Nation and humanity. The key to this effort is the coordination of qualified, experienced & dedicated faculty members of our department who imparts quality education to our students. We help our students to gain knowledge in Current technologies & be clear focused. With Emerging technology, Design & Innovations our department has produced good quality Professional in IT sectors.
